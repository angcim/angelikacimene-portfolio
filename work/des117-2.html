Portal Page 2018

This was a portal page created in 2018 for my HTML and CSS class. This portal page links to my porfolio, my blog, and all the exercises I did throughout the quarter. The header was created in Adobe Illustrator which represents me as a designer. My banner and icons were drawn and created through Illustrator.

For this portal page, I attempted to incorporate responsive designs for smaller and mobile screens. As the screen gets smaller, the icons decrease in size, my banner disappears, and all the content is in one column instead of two. These choices will help better the experience of a mobile user.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator and Atom

Graphica

This was another one of my projects for my UI/UX class. The project was to create a website with the topic that was given to me: Education.

Graphica is an educational website that offers online courses and design projects. The targeted audience includes undergraduate and post-graduates who want to enhance their design skills. The website includes a search in the homepage for get the users started on what they want to learn. Graphica also includes an online portfolio review for users who want to improve their portfolios for jobs and interviews.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop

Wayfinding and Signage System for California Hall

The objective of the project is to create a signage system for California Hall. The theme for this project is based on the abundance of fruits and vegetables in California. UC Davis is known for its agriculture, therefore California is a great canvas to tell its story.

This project contains extensive research on the building itself, the audience, and California's agriculture. California Hall is a new building in UC Davis. The interior is filled with white walls and wooden panels. We used this open canvas as an opportunity to fill it with lots of vibrant colors.

Programs Used: Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign

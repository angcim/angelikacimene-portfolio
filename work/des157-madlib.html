Adopt a Dog MadLib

The objective of the assignment was to create a MadLib.

During the assignment, we had work with Javascript to process a form. The form itself was created through HTML, but in order for the users to submit and input their answers, we had to incorporate Javascript.

My madlib is called Adopt a Dog to promote awareness of adopting dogs from dog shelters rather than from breeders. My design choices in illustration, color, and typography helps my madlib fun, engaging, and friendly.

How it works: Fill in the form based on the label and press submit or if the user wants to start over, press reset. In the madlib, the choices of the breed and item will determine what animation the viewer will receive. This is to help entertain the audience with a variety of results each time they fill in the form.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe AfterEffects, and Atom

Go Nuts on Mix'D

This project was created in a branding and package course I took over the summer. The objective of this project is to pick any dried goods from the grocery store and redesign it. The constraint was that the packaging will incorporate biomimicry and must cater to the eldery.

MIX'D is an organic trail mix company that advocates for a healthy diet and lifestyle. I wanted my brand to be playful and friendly to illustrate that eating healthy can be fun and exciting.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign
